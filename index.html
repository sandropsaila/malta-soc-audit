Audit Frameworks

SOC 2 (ISAE 3000)
Evaluates security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy controls. Type I assesses design; Type II assesses operating effectiveness over 6–12 months. International equivalent of ISAE 3000.
SOC 1
Assesses internal controls relevant to clients' financial reporting. Governed by SSAE 18 (US) or ISAE 3402 (international). Issued as Type I (point-in-time) or Type II (period-of-time).
ISAE 3402
The international equivalent of SOC 1. Provides assurance on service-organisation controls relevant to user entities' financial reporting. Widely used in EU financial services, payroll, and fund administration.
ISAE 3000
The overarching international assurance standard for non-financial engagements, covering information security, GDPR, ESG, and more. SOC 2 reports issued internationally typically comply with ISAE 3000.
