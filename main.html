Audit Frameworks

SOC 2 (ISAE 3000)
Evaluates security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy controls. Type I assesses design; Type II assesses operating effectiveness over 6–12 months.
SOC 1
Assesses internal controls relevant to clients' financial reporting. Governed by SSAE 18 (US) or ISAE 3402 (international). Issued as Type I or Type II.
ISAE 3402
The international equivalent of SOC 1. Provides assurance on service-organisation controls relevant to user entities' financial reporting. Widely used in EU financial services.
ISAE 3000
The overarching international assurance standard for non-financial engagements, covering information security, GDPR, ESG, and more. SOC 2 reports issued internationally typically comply with ISAE 3000.

MFSA Regulatory Context

⚖️ MFSA Investment Services Rules — System Certification Obligation

Under Rule R1-1.12 (System Certification) of Part BI and Rule R13-13.6 (CFD/Rolling Spot Forex Trading Organisations), MFSA-licensed investment firms must ensure electronic trading systems undergo independent assessment by a qualified third party before live operation and after material updates. Acceptable formats include ISAE 3000, ISAE 3402, SOC 2, PCI DSS QSA audits, and bespoke regulatory assurance engagements. Payment institutions licensed under the Financial Institutions Act (FIR/02, FIR/03) are subject to equivalent IT audit obligations.

Sources: MFSA Part BI Rules (Dec 2024) · Part A: Application Process (Oct 2025)
