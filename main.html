MFSA Regulatory Context

Investment Services Rules · System Certification Obligation
Rule R1-1.12 & Rule R13-13.6 — Independent Assessment Requirement

Under Rule R1-1.12 (System Certification) of Part BI of the MFSA Investment Services Rules for MiFID Firms, and Rule R13-13.6 (Supplementary Rules for CFD/Rolling Spot Forex Trading Organisations), licence holders must ensure electronic trading systems undergo independent assessment by a qualified third party before live operation and following material updates. Acceptable formats include ISAE 3000, ISAE 3402, SOC 2, PCI DSS QSA audits, and bespoke regulatory assurance engagements.

Payment institutions licensed under the Financial Institutions Act (FIR/02, FIR/03) are subject to equivalent IT audit obligations. Rule 14.6 of Part A applies analogous system certification requirements to Binary Options providers.

Sources: MFSA Part BI Rules (Dec 2024) · Part A: Application Process (Oct 2025)

Methodology & Sources

Data Sources

Company data was cross-referenced against the Tech.mt Members Directory (556 companies), the MGA Licensee Register (575 licensees), and the MFSA Investment Services Licence Holders register. Certification data was retrieved from official corporate announcements, LinkedIn company pages (including pages not indexed by search engines), press releases, and news portals. SOC 2 and ISAE 3402 reports are confidential by nature and typically shared only under NDA; this registry reflects only publicly disclosed information. PCI DSS Level 1 QSA audits are treated as equivalent to ISAE 3402 for payment processing control assurance. Last updated May 2026 — not for vendor due-diligence without independent verification.
